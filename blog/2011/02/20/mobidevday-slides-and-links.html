MobiDevDay Slides and Links

20 February 2011

Had a spectacular time at MobiDevDay yesterday. I ended up doing the PhoneGap presentation twice in a row to packed rooms. The crowds were friendly, asked great questions, and many of them said they would take the next step to look into PhoneGap for their projects.

Many thanks to Nitobi for sending the shirts! I flaked and gave them all away, and didn't save one for myself.
