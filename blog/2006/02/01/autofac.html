Autofac

1 February 2006

Last night one of the repossession agents at my old company was shot. He took a bullet in the bicep which then exited out his shoulder. He is alive and in hospital. Not suprisingly, the news has no mention of this, despite the arrest of three men involved in the incident.

When I think my problems are too large for me to handle, that what I am going through is too difficult, too daunting, for me to complete, I remember the things I have seen and lived through, and I know that I will keep going.

I will be in Charlotte, NC for iWPS until Sunday. I hope being there will put me in a better mood. I look forward to seeing many of you there.
